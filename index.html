Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to general questions about our processes, timelines, materials, and warranty.

How much does an interior design project typically cost?

The cost varies based on materials, layout scope, and project scale. We offer customized budgets starting from basic modular kitchens to complete premium villa makeovers. Book a free consultation for an itemized quote.

What is the typical project timeline from start to finish?

Our standard production and installation timeframe is 40 working days from final design sign-off. The conceptual design phase can take between 7 to 15 days depending on revisions.

What materials do you use for your projects?

We source premium plywood (IS:710 boiling waterproof), acrylic and laminate finishes, and modular kitchen hardware from trusted German and global brands like Hettich, Blum, and Hafele.

Do you offer warranty on your work?

Yes, we provide a 5-year warranty on modular cabinets and modular kitchen setups, and manufacturer-backed warranties on brand accessories and hardware accessories.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Vellore, Chennai, and adjoining districts, with physical offices situated conveniently in Egmore (Chennai) and Katpadi Road (Vellore) for material previews.
